# Copywriting audit

## The job of the two pages

The homepage helps a new visitor answer four questions in order.

1. What does Jai help me do?
2. Why should I trust his judgment?
3. What have other people done with his help?
4. What will I leave with if I book a session?

The About page explains why Jai does this work, how his background shapes the method, and how paid guidance supports the path toward a collective by June 2027.

## About page audit

| Section                        | Current problem                                                                                          | New job                                                                                                           |
| ------------------------------ | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Hero                           | The role labels are clear. The paragraph can state the practical result more directly.                   | Explain that Jai helps a person choose the first useful project and make the next decision without him.           |
| Experience rail                | The proof is specific and easy to scan.                                                                  | Keep it.                                                                                                          |
| The in between period          | The facts are strong. A few lines rely on abstract phrases such as a practice lasting beyond a calendar. | State the current funding model and June 2027 aim in literal language.                                            |
| Choose the job before the tool | The method is the strongest differentiated section.                                                      | Keep the structure. Tighten the opening line and preserve the six choices.                                        |
| Shakti                         | The section proves that Jai can leave AI out of a critical path.                                         | Keep it and state why that choice protects the public result.                                                     |
| Roadmap                        | The phrase "seeds the public work" is visually attractive but vague.                                     | Say that paid guidance funds the public work and explain what is available now versus what is still being tested. |
| Offer                          | The artifact and limit are clear.                                                                        | Keep the structure and use the same wording as the homepage CTA.                                                  |
| Closing                        | The invitation is clear and low pressure.                                                                | Keep it.                                                                                                          |

## Proof policy

- Use the nine video testimonials that are currently published on chaiwithjai.com.
- Do not claim that there are eleven until two more published videos can be verified.
- Link every case study to its full page on chaiwithjai.com.
- Describe case study numbers as participant reported outcomes unless the underlying measurement record is public.
- Keep Shakti results separate from coaching outcomes.
- Keep the technical vlog available, but do not use it as the main proof for the guidance offer.
