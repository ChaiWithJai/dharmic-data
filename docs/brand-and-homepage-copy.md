# Dharmic Data brand and homepage copy

## Brand idea

Dharmic Data is Jai Bhagat's AI guidance practice and public lab. Jai helps people choose a useful AI project, test it against their work, and decide what is worth building next. The public lab includes working projects and the evidence behind them.

The name refers to service and responsibility. It does not claim authority over a faith or culture.

## Voice

The voice is clear and generous.

We speak as builders who show our work. We name the person a project may help. We explain the data and the limits. We invite people to try the work and state when it is unfinished.

Use simple words and complete sentences. Say `we tested 150 fixed building records` instead of `the system is reliable`. Say `the public demo does not use AI` instead of `AI powered civic intelligence`.

## Tone by situation

For an invitation, be warm and specific. Use `Try the building record demo`.

For proof, be direct. State the date, test, result, and limit.

For an error, say what happened and what the person can do next. Use `We could not reach the City data service. Try this address again in a few minutes.`

For a plan, use `planned` and name the next test. Do not present planned work as available work.
